Did you know you can buy a car and import it home to Zimbabwe

Here is how
  1. Contact us and tell us the type of car you want.
  2. We source that car for you and if you like it we verify and make the car ready.
  3. You make a direct payment to the seller through the bank either in Japan or South Africa depending where the car is located.
  4. We present you with the paperwork and we arrange for the duty payment.
  5. After the duty has been payed, we then make sure the car is transported to Zimbabwe for the final paperwork.
  6. After the paperwork the car will be ready to collection.
